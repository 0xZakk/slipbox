### Highlights

- “Open in New Tab”
- You can easily access and operate tabs via keyboard shortcuts as well:
  ⌘T (cmd-T) to open a new tab 
  ⌃⇥ (control-tab) will switch to the tab on the right 
  ⌃⇧⇥ (control-shift-tab) opens the tab on the left
